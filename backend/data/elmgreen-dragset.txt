Elmgreen and Dragset are a duo of artists who work at the crossroads of art and architecture, performance and installation. They are Michael Elmgreen, born in Copenhagen, Denmark in 1961, and Ingar Dragset, born in Trondheim, Norway in 1969. They met in Copenhagen in 1994 and started collaborating in 1995. They live and work in Berlin, Germany. They are known for their humorous and subversive works that challenge the conventions of art institutions and public spaces.
